ARNAVD

House before hoes.

Romain "Arnavd" Arnavd is a 23 y.o. web developper from Montpellier, France, who just started making house music as a hobby.

Influenced by the French Touch scene and the likes of Tchami, Malaa, Dustycloud, Tony Romera and all the others incredible producers out there, he wanted to make his own Bass House musics.

In the end, he began to focus more and more on the Tech House scene since the beginning of 2019, and he's not going to stop.
