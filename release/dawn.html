Dawn

EP • 2018

My first EP, marking the start of my Arnavd project. I called it "Dawn" because it was the EP symbolizing the dawn of my project.
For the cover art, I was hugely inspired by the cover of "Adieu" by Tchami, so I decided to make a cover art with the same look, but using a picture I took of the Piton de la Fournaise, the volcano in the Reunion Island.
