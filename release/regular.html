Regular

Single • 2018

The first single to come after my debut EP, Regular, is a chill House song I decided to make for the summer. I got the main inspiration from "Buggin'" by Hot Since 82 (the pluck melody, the pads, the chill vocals and the intro were the main parts I got the inspiration from).
For the cover art, I wanted to make something that felt happy so I went for a cyan and pink background with a cloud texture. On top of that, I put a greek statue with pink neons lighting it (I got the inspiration for this from "Back to the fire" by Fatesky)
